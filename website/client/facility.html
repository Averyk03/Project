About our facilities

Ace Sports Hub offer a wide range of athletic and meeting facility, many of which are availabele for rental for resonable price. Rental fees very by facility, and availability may be limited during summer (June through August) due to summer camp. See bottom of page for fee information.

Please note that we receive a very high volume of requests every day. Kindly allow two working days for us to confirm your booking request. You will be able to submit a request up to 2 months in advance. The booking details and information will be provided 2 weeks before the booking.
